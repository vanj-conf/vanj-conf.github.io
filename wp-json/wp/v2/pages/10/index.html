VANJ Conference 2021<\/strong> (VANJ2021<\/strong>) – Bridging Academic and Industry<\/strong> is organized by the Vietnamese Academic Network in Japan (VANJ) and partnered with Vietnam Youth Vet Network (VYVN) and Vietnam Biomedical Science Community (VBMC), following the success of the previous VANJ2020 – Science and Technology in the New Normal. VANJ2021<\/strong> is a multidisciplinary platform for science and technology exchange events with the participation of scientists, researchers, and intellectuals.<\/span><\/p>\n

<\/span><\/p>\n

This year, VANJ2021<\/strong> emphasizes the importance of coordinating and deepening the linkages between academic and industry in order to facilitate the effective use by society of the results of university-based research. VANJ2021 welcomes all the scientists, researchers, experts and intellectuals to jointly participate and discuss \u201cBridging Academic and Industry<\/strong>\u201d in various aspects of education, science and technology. With high-quality and in-depth scientific content, VANJ2021<\/strong> also aims to promote active research collaborations between Vietnam and Japan.<\/span><\/p>\n

<\/span><\/p>\n

Participants of this conference are mainly researchers from Japan and Vietnam, but researchers from other regions are warmly welcome.<\/span><\/p>\n
