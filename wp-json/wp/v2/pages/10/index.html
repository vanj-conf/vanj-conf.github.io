VANJ Conference 2021<\/strong> (VANJ2021<\/strong>) – Bridging Academic and Industry<\/strong> is organized by the Vietnamese Academic Network in Japan (VANJ) and partnered with Vietnam Youth Vet Network (VYVN) and Vietnam Biomedical Science Community (VBMC), following the success of the previous VANJ2020 – Science and Technology in the New Normal. VANJ2021<\/strong> is a multidisciplinary platform for science and technology exchange events with the participation of scientists, researchers, and intellectuals.<\/span><\/p>\n<\/span>\n

This year, VANJ2021<\/strong> emphasizes the importance of coordinating and deepening the linkages between academic and industry in order to facilitate the effective use by society of the results of university-based research. VANJ2021 welcomes all the scientists, researchers, experts and intellectuals to jointly participate and discuss \u201cBridging Academic and Industry<\/strong>\u201d in various aspects of education, science and technology. With high-quality and in-depth scientific content, VANJ2021<\/strong> also aims to promote active research collaborations between Vietnam and Japan.<\/span><\/p>\n<\/span>\n

Participants of this conference are mainly researchers from Japan and Vietnam, but researchers from other regions are warmly welcome.<\/span><\/p>\n<\/span>\n

VANJ2021<\/strong> includes Special Sessions to discuss the following scientific areas:<\/span><\/p>\n<\/span>\n

\u2605\u00a0 Bio-Medical Sciences and Public Health<\/span><\/p>\n<\/span>\n

\u2605\u00a0 Veterinary Medical Science<\/span><\/p>\n<\/span>\n

\u2605\u00a0 Clean-Renewable Energy and Smart-Technology for Power Networks<\/span><\/p>\n<\/span>\n

\u2605\u00a0 Micro\/Nano Science and Material Science<\/span><\/p>\n<\/span>\n

\u2605\u00a0 Policies and Economic Strategies<\/span><\/p>\n<\/span>\n

We also welcome submissions from all general scientific topics (e.g., Basic sciences, Electrical Engineering, Environmental Engineering, AI, ICT, IoT, and Robotics), especially for young scientists and students for General Sessions. This year, VANJ2021 will provide two main awards for general presentations: Excellent Oral Presentations and Excellent Poster Presentations.<\/span><\/p>\n\n\n
